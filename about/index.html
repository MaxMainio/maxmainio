About

I am Max Mainio Beidler, a Graphic Designer whose work lives at the intersection of tradition and technology. As a dual citizen of Finland and the United States, I carry with me the perspective of two cultures — and while I reside on the West Coast today, Helsinki, Finland, will always be my home.

My curiosity lies in exploring how emerging tools — from generative design methods and procedural systems to artificial intelligence and variable typography — can expand the possibilities of visual expression. At the same time, I remain grounded in the value of craft, materiality, and design traditions that give work depth and meaning.

I am motivated by the idea that design is never static. It is an evolving field where experimentation, innovation, and cultural perspective come together to imagine what doesn’t yet exist.
