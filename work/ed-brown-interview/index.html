Ed Brown Interview

Interview print and publication / 2022

For this assignment, all of the juniors in graphic design were asked: How do you use text and image to tell someone’s story? How does the content, aesthetics, and tone fit into an established publication?

We all had the opportunity to reach out and interview any designer, artist, author, or other person of interest. Then together as a grade compile our interviews into a newspaper style publication introducing our person of interest and their work to the rest of the school.
