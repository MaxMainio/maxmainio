Moss Growth Simulation

Mapping group project / Physical Map / 2021

In this physical map I was focused on recreating and simulating the natural over growth of the moss and other urban vegetation that was growing through and around the bricks in the sidewalk. Scroll to see how it changes over time.

I also created a Phenomenal map in which I mapped out the temporality of the intersection and it's many interweaving parts.
