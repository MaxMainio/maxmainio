Moss Growth Simulation

Mapping group project / Physical Map / 2021

I created the map you see here on the right, in which I was interested in the physicality of the moss and other vegitation that was growing through and around the bricks in the sidewalk. Scroll to see how it changes over time.

I also created a Phenomenal map in which I mapped out the temporality of the intersection and it's many interweaving parts.
