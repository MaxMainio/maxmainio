Crosswalk Poetics

Mapping group project / 2021

For this project Vincent Li, Ruby Miller, and I studied a cross walk located at College st & Main st Providence Rhode Island. Our goal was to create methods of mapping various aspects of the location:

Physical, Phenomenal, History + Use, Sensorial, & Personal / Poetic / Subjective

We divvied up the various aspects of mapping between our selves based off of what each of us were most interested in, and where our strengths lie. I focused on the physical and phenomenal aspects.

We would reconvene throughout this process to share together what we had created and establish how to continue the project. Through this iterative process of mapping our location we ultimately created an interactive installation. A large blank book on a table into which we projected poems, randomly generated from a library of both visual and literary samples we curated through our mapping process.

Here you can see a video of the final installation along with the final version of both my physical & phenomenal maps.
