Who are we?

We are the Global Customer Service hotline. We can help you fix any issue you might be encountering. Anything from illness, environmental problems, psychological distress, to helping you fix your broken refridgerator.

Originally established in 2016 as a subdivision of the Ministry of General Affairs of the Government of the Netherlands. Quickly it became apparent to all how influential a "swiss army knife" style governmental body could be.

So, in November of 2022 it was deccided at the UN to expand our project into an international effort to help not only the Dutch, but the entire world. Now, with funding from all 193 UN member states we have only become better at helping those in need.

How do we help you? It's easy, just scroll a little further and select one of the categories below to start a chat with one of our specialists who will begin to help you with your problem. And before you know it, you will be problem free!
