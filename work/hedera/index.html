Hedera

Experimental Font Genus / 2023

The third and final part of my degree project took a different angle. Rather than pitting human against nature or vice versa, I sought harmony. The human sans serif font “genus” Hedera, with a standardized and minimalist appearance takes on a chaotic and organic life through a avant-garde approach to the use of Opentype features.

At the heart of the Hedera genus is an evolution that mirrors the natural world. Drawing inspiration from the resilient growth of ivy plants, the typeface exhibits an organic dynamism. As one types, the letters don’t merely follow one another but instead interlace, intertwine, and overlap. These dynamic connections make the text come alive, reminiscent of flora seeking its path, wrapping and climbing structures. It’s this intricate dance—a collaboration between the methodical strokes of human design and the wild, unrestrained spirit of nature—that defines the Hedera genus. Rather than adhering to convention, this genus of typefaces revels in its organic unpredictability—it was never about creating the perfect font for your resume.
