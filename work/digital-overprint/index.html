Digital Overprint

Print and scan feedback experiment / 2021

For this experiment I printed out an AI generated face I had made in the past. I printed it at 300dpi at four varying sizes: 1in2, 2in2, 4in2, & 8in2. I then scanned each at 1200ppi, in Photoshop I scaled them all to the same size, overlayed them and used different blending modes to mix them together.

I was Intrigued by the fractals that would emerge from the interaction of the varying sized ink halftone dot patterns of the prints.

Click & drag around the images above to see how they interact.Sadly the original print scan files are too large (189.6MB) to use on this site, so I had to downsize them.
